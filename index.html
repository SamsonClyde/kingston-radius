How to Use Kingston Radius

Everything you need to know to find your next show.

Browsing Shows

The Music page lists upcoming live performances from over 100 venues within 90 minutes of Kingston, NY. Shows are sorted chronologically.

Filtering by Distance

Use the distance buttons to narrow shows by how far you want to travel:

  • Kingston only — venues right in Kingston, NY
  • ≤ 30 min — Rosendale, Woodstock, Saugerties, Bearsville area
  • ≤ 60 min — Hudson, New Paltz, Poughkeepsie, Rhinebeck, Newburgh
  • ≤ 90 min — Peekskill, Bethel Woods, North Adams MA and beyond

Filtering by Month

Click any month button to see only shows in that month. Click it again to return to all months.

Filtering by Venue

Use the venue dropdown to see only shows from a specific venue — great for checking what's coming up at a favorite spot.

Venue & Ticket Links

Each event card shows the venue name as a link to that venue's website or calendar, and the city name links to Google Maps directions. Click through to buy tickets or confirm details directly with the venue.

About Kingston Radius

A live music calendar for the Hudson Valley — built for people who actually go to shows.

What This Is

Kingston Radius is an independent concert listing site covering live music within roughly 90 minutes of Kingston, NY.

Why It Exists

Finding out what's happening locally shouldn't mean checking a dozen different venue websites, Facebook events, and Instagram stories. Kingston Radius is one place for the region's live music scene — or that is the goal at least, but nothing beats a flyer on a telephone pole.

Coverage Area

The site covers venues from Kingston and Woodstock at its core, expanding outward to Hudson, New Paltz, Poughkeepsie, Newburgh, Peekskill, Bethel Woods, the Catskills, and into western Massachusetts.
