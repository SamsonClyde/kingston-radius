How to Use Kingston Radius

Everything you need to know to find your next show.

Browsing Shows

The Music page lists upcoming live performances from over 50 venues within 90 minutes of Kingston, NY. Shows are sorted chronologically and the listings refresh automatically throughout the day.

Filtering by Distance

Use the distance buttons to narrow shows by how far you want to travel:

  • Kingston only — venues right in Kingston, NY
  • ≤ 30 min — Rosendale, Woodstock, Saugerties, Bearsville area
  • ≤ 60 min — Hudson, New Paltz, Poughkeepsie, Rhinebeck, Newburgh
  • ≤ 90 min — Peekskill, Bethel Woods, North Adams MA and beyond

Filtering by Month

Click any month button to see only shows in that month. Click it again to return to all months. The buttons are generated automatically based on which months have upcoming events in the data.

Filtering by Venue

Use the venue dropdown to see only shows from a specific venue — great for checking what's coming up at a favorite spot.

Free Shows

The Free only toggle filters to no-cover events. Free status is pulled from each venue's listing and may not always be fully up to date — when in doubt, check with the venue directly.

Venue & Ticket Links

Each event card shows the venue name as a link to that venue's website or calendar, and the city name links to Google Maps directions. Click through to buy tickets or confirm details directly with the venue.

Tip: Don't see something you know is happening? Use the ⚙ button in the bottom-right corner to add events manually. They appear instantly with a red left border.

Adding Events Manually

Press the ⚙ button on any page. After entering the admin password, fill in the event title, date, time, venue, and price. Manually added events are saved in your browser and persist between visits. The Manage tab lets you remove them, and the Export button downloads a backup file.

Venues That Only Announce by Email or Instagram

Some local venues — like Night Swim, Utility Bicycle Works, Left Bank Ciders, and others — announce shows only through email newsletters or Instagram rather than a public calendar. For those, the fastest workflow is:

  • Open the newsletter or post when it arrives
  • Hit ⚙ on Kingston Radius and add the event using the form — takes about 20 seconds
  • It will show up immediately in the listings alongside everything else

About Kingston Radius

A live music calendar for the Hudson Valley — built for people who actually go to shows.

What This Is

Kingston Radius is an independent concert listing site covering live music within roughly 90 minutes of Kingston, NY. It pulls event data from venues across the Hudson Valley and puts it all in one clean, fast-loading calendar, updated continuously throughout the day.

Why It Exists

Finding out what's happening locally shouldn't mean checking a dozen different venue websites, Facebook events, and Instagram stories. Kingston Radius is one place for the region's live music scene.

Coverage Area

The site covers venues from Kingston and Woodstock at its core, expanding outward to Hudson, New Paltz, Poughkeepsie, Newburgh, Peekskill, Bethel Woods, the Catskills, and into western Massachusetts. If a venue is within roughly 90 minutes of downtown Kingston and has a public calendar, it's either already listed or in the queue to add.

Venues Currently Listed

Kingston Radius currently tracks events from over 50 venues including Tubby's, Assembly Kingston, Unicorn Bar, Keegan Ales, Bearsville Theater, Levon Helm Studios, Basilica Hudson, Bardavon, UPAC, Opus 40, The Ashokan Center, The Lemon Squeeze, The Falcon, Paramount Hudson Valley, City Winery Hudson Valley, MASS MoCA, MONUMENT hv, Reher Center, and many more.

Missing a Venue?

If there's a venue you'd like to see added — or if you spot incorrect information — use the admin panel to add events manually in the meantime.

Kingston Radius is an independent local project. It is not affiliated with, sponsored by, or endorsed by any of the venues listed. All event information links back directly to the originating venue.
